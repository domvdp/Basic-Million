This is the most important part of becoming financially free, period.

The mindset section will include content aimed towards enlightening you about a very simple yet vital fact, that you don't need to spend much money to be happy. Many things that result in true happiness are often free, or are at least cheaper than you expected.

If you can master being able to save more than you spend for the rest of your life whilst maintaining your normal levels of happiness, then this alone will drastically change your life for the better.

So, lets get to it! List out all those monthly subscriptions and cancel those that are not essiential to your happiness. Do you really need to buy a new car? Are you sure you need to buy a bigger house? Do you actually need that brand new 4K TV? Can you reduce your rent? Does your child really need so many presents, or would they prefer an adventure or more parental interaction?

In this SAVING's section, I am going to break down what you can do to save money without reducing the quality of your life.

Important note - do not comprimise your health to save money! Invest in yourself by eating healthily and exercising. This is the one expense that can easily be justified.
